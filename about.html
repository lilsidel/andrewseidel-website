About Me

I am a sophmore at Brigham Young Univserity. I am majoring in accounting and plan to graduate in the spring of 2027. I come from Kissimmee a small town in Florida just south of Orlando. I am the oldest with four little sisters.

Throughout high school I started in three varsity sports: Football, Basketball, Lacrosse, and I currently am part of the mens lacrosse team at Brigham Young Univeristy. Growing up in sports taught me many key skills I use everyday. I learned how to work with others, discipline, and hardwork. It also has inspired me to give back to the community and help the younger generation by coahing and mentoring.

I served an LDS mission in Calgary Canada and spoke spanish in my time there. I gained so much experience and learned to care for others in a way I did not think was possible before. Through this experience I gained a grew my testimony to be a better person and a disciple of Heavenly Father
